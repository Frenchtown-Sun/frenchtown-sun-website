title: "Reserve Borough Park for personal use"
edition: "2026-05"
weight: 2
layout: "article"
---
Borough residents may reserve the use of Borough Park, located on Creek Road. The park can be a busy place during the warm weather months, so residents are encouraged to apply early. The application can be found at the Borough website and then emailed to [clerk@frenchtownboro.gov](mailto:clerk@frenchtownboro.gov) or mailed to the borough clerk at Borough Hall.
